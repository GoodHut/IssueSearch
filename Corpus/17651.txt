 URL : "https://github.com/hughsie/fwupd/issues/181" TITLE : ram usage is high BODY : fwupd eats about 44mb on my system, adjusted for shared binaries. this is not a lot in our crazy times of multi-gb laptops, but for something used 0.00001% of the time it's on a high side. much bigger daemons that are used much more frequently, but consume less ram: libvirtd: 15mb bittorrent sync dropbox equivalent : 30mb compiz: 37mb nautilus: 15mb
