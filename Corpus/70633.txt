 URL : "https://github.com/voxpupuli/puppet-misp/issues/6" TITLE : workflow in this module BODY : hi, on vox pupuli we work with the github-workflow, or pull requests. this means: you fork the module, create a feature/bugfix branch, make your changes, push it to your fork, create a pull request, somebody reviews it and then it gets merged into master. we don't directly push to master. and we try to not create feature/bugfix branches in the vox pupuli namespace. ---- i disabled direct pushes to master in this repository. if you've any questions feel free to respond here or join our irc channel voxpupuli on freenode.
