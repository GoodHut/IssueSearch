 URL : "https://github.com/jn9cn/TodoApp/issues/2" TITLE : suggestion - indentation BODY : tabs are old school but some people still use them . the more popular way is to use spaces. this has the advantage of looking more consistent across editors. most editors support soft tabs which makes your tab key insert the appropriate amount of spaces. 2 spaces per tab or 4 spaces per tab are the most common choices.
