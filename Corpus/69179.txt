 URL : "https://github.com/ijlyttle/AzureOAuth/issues/1" TITLE : suggest permission link BODY : one way a web api token-request can fail happens when the app has not been approved by a tenant administrator. we can detect this failure, then compose and offer this link that can be forwarded to a tenant admin; she can click the link to approve the app. https://login.microsoftonline.com/{tenant_id}/adminconsent?client_id={application_id}&state=12345&redirect_uri=http://localhost/
