 URL : "https://github.com/DRBJAX/prj-rev-bwfs-dasmoto/issues/4" TITLE : using group selectors BODY : simplify! consider adding a group selector for any repeated styles in your css. for example, font-family: helvetica; is repeated a few times throughout your css. instead of repeating this style you should use a group selector for it to make your css more concise. you could use: h1, h2, span { font-family: helvetica; } then delete font-family: helvetica; from those individual selectors. this practice helps keep your css concise.
