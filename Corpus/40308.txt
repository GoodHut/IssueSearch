 URL : "https://github.com/kabivel/Sins-of-Empires/issues/73" TITLE : trade port income op BODY : currently there exists a research called expanded cargoholds which, when fully researched, increases the income generated by trade hub by ~1500%. this number is far too high. stacking planets full of trade ports is now a necessary strategy, as with only a few planets a player can generate 200+ credits per second. i believe this value should be reduced by 50-70%. with this done, the economy still relies on trade hubs, but does not generate a ridiculous amount of cred.
