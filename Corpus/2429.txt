 URL : "https://github.com/yakyak/yakyak/issues/801" TITLE : add lock file BODY : without a package-lock.json and/or yarn.lock file, any kind of breaking update to a dependency could break a freshly cloned copy of the repo. best practices suggest adding a lock. see this stack overflow question https://stackoverflow.com/questions/44552348/should-i-commit-yarn-lock-and-package-lock-json-files
