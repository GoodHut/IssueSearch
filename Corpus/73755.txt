 URL : "https://github.com/DNSOP/draft-ietf-dnsop-terminology-bis/issues/20" TITLE : definition for root zone BODY : the current definition is: the zone whose apex is the zero-length label. also sometimes called the dns root . this definition is clearly dns-centric because of the zero-length ; other domain name systems might have the root zone have a name. the second sentence also makes it dns-centric. proposal 1 not dns-centric : the zone at the apex of a domain name tree. proposal 2 dns-centric : the zone of in dns-based tree whose apex is the zero-length label. also sometimes called the dns root .
