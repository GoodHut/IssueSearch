 URL : "https://github.com/spoqa/spoqa-han-sans/issues/98" TITLE : tweak @font-face rule BODY : - current font-face rule https://github.com/spoqa/spoqa-han-sans/blob/master/css/spoqahansans-kr.css has precedence of eot - svg - ttf - woff, which is causing ttf to be downloaded for modern browsers. i believe use of svg fonts is discouraged nowadays, so modern browsers ignore it even though its high precedence - whenever necessary, woff2 is favorable but it is not described in @font-face rule. i suggest it to be changed to the top one deepest possible browser support in https://css-tricks.com/snippets/css/using-font-face/
