 URL : "https://github.com/PeterGerrard/WPF.Accessibility.Testing/issues/5" TITLE : don't hard-code colors BODY : people who are color blind, have low vision, or are using a black and white screen might not be able to use applications with hard-coded colors. following this best practice allows users to adjust color combinations based on individual needs. https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/framework/ui-automation/accessibility-best-practices dont-hard-code-colors
