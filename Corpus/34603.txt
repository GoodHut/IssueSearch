 URL : "https://github.com/Dev-Outreach/html-portfolio/issues/12" TITLE : colour choice sample BODY : create a sample to show how poor colour choice can impact usability. this does not need an improved sample, the goal here is to foster awareness and discussion around overlooked areas of design. pretty much a site that looks good but is hard to use for some people due to colour scheme choices. an example would be a class selection calendar that uses red/green for open/close days. see the following pages for refrence: poor contrast: http://www.etre.com/tools/colourcheck/ color blindness: http://www.etre.com/tools/colourblindsimulator/ http://colorfilter.wickline.org/
