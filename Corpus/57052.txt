 URL : "https://github.com/rnambis/SE17-group-O/issues/4" TITLE : personalized news feed BODY : a low bandwidth news app where a user can choose his preferences of news. we have a designed three solutions for the same, 1. web application: we are planning to create a web application in python flask that allows the user to create an account and view latest news according to his preferences. 2. a news reader: a text-to-speech generator that can read the news to the user. 3. a text-based news delivery system: news delivered in the form of text message, particularly targeted in users with limited internet accessibility.
