 URL : "https://github.com/masak/007/issues/231" TITLE : make regexes a slang BODY : regexes stand out to me as the slang: a special-purpose dsl within 007/perl 6 for matching strings. with its own closed universe of q nodes and its own execution semantics backtracking . furthermore, values enter and values leave. it screams slang. because of that, i think it'd be good for us to make it a slang early , simply because it's the most obvious one. use cases that are helpful to the regex slang are likely to be helpful to other slangs too.
