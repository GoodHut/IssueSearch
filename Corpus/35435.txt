 URL : "https://github.com/TelenavMapping/mapping-projects/issues/32" TITLE : ecuador lane editing BODY : description this project is designed to improve the quality of osm by adding and updating lanes and turn lanes on motorways, trunk, primary, secondary roads and corresponding links in ecuador. we will focus on the following cities: quito, guayaquil, cuenca, ambato, portoviejo, machala, loja and on major and minor routes https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/highways_in_ecuador . how to map for editing lanes and turn lanes we will use josm , the turnlanes-tagging editor https://www.mapbox.com/blog/turn-lanes-mapping/ plugin and the lane and road attributes map paint style. we will use the ‘lanes= ’ tag to add the number of lanes on every way as it is described here http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/lanes . for the direction in which a lane will lead we will use the ‘turn:lanes= ’ tag as it is described here http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/key:turn turning_indications_per_lane . sources we will use mainly the street level imagery : osc, mapillary but also where is possible, aerial imagery : bing, digital globe, esri world imagery who will be involved telenav mapping team https://github.com/telenavmapping/mapping-projects/wiki/team see also http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/lanes http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/key:turn turning_indications_per_lane https://github.com/josm/turnlanes-tagging https://www.mapbox.com/blog/turn-lanes-mapping/
