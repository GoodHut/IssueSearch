 URL : "https://github.com/osvaldasvalutis/imageLightbox.js/issues/9" TITLE : incompatible with jquery 3 BODY : the plugin seems not to be compatible with jquery 3. this is probably due to the this.selector property accessed in the plugin, which is not available anymore with jquery 3. see https://api.jquery.com/selector/ for reference. here is a discussion on an alternative approach to this.selector : https://stackoverflow.com/questions/21238281/what-replaces-the-deprecated-property-this-selector do you consider on making the plugin compatible with jquery 3.
