 URL : "https://github.com/Mo90mo/prj-rev-bwfs-dasmoto/issues/3" TITLE : locally stored images BODY : simplify! consider using locally stored images in your project as this is best practice for production code and will make your page load faster. to do this you would create a new images folder inside your resources folder, like this: ! screen shot 2017-09-20 at 7 16 26 am https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/11949479/30649637-a83781e2-9dd5-11e7-825d-0f39fe675bc6.png then save your images to this new folder, and use your <img> tags like this: <img src= ./resources/images/hacksaw.jpeg alt= image of hacksaw paint brushes > also, be sure to use descriptive alt attributes on your <img> elements see above for example . this helps with accessiblity and is best practice as well. example: https://github.com/mo90mo/prj-rev-bwfs-dasmoto/blob/master/dasmoto/index.html l10
