 URL : "https://github.com/Openarl/PathOfBuilding/issues/160" TITLE : max dps after ramp up BODY : certain situations like multiple poison stacks or multiple ignites aren't being taken into account when calculating dps. lets say i have a dps of 20000 and my poison dps is 4000. based on my attack/cast rate i'll apply multiple poisons. for instance if i have a 3 second poison duration and a 0.5 attack/cast rate, i'll be able to apply 5 concurrent poisons so my max dps which would take 3 seconds to ramp would be dps + poison dps number of concurrent poisons
