BODY : it might be beneficial to start a proper branching strategy, for example do development on a develop branch. then you could make use of github's web hosting service. for example, instead of always uploading the files to a new directory on you webspace, just merge and commit to your master branch and push the changes - automatically making everything available for players. i took the liberty of doing so for my fork. check out https://project-am.github.io/bondage-college/
