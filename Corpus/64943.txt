 URL : "https://github.com/fabd/kanji-koohii/issues/11" TITLE : https secure connection BODY : signing in to any website without a secure connection isn't a good thing. google are planning to present users with a red warning sign https://security.googleblog.com/2016/09/moving-towards-more-secure-web.html on all sites that are not on a secure connection in the future. there are two alternatives here as far as i see: 1. buy a certificate and use that 2. configure let's encrypt with the website buying a certificate is slightly easier but using lc is cheaper and can be really handy when you set it up to renew automatically.
