 URL : "https://github.com/jandrewtorres/BioWars/issues/19" TITLE : fr: buy virus BODY : if the user buy a virus through the store, the system shall check the user’s remaining golds. if the amount of golds user have is larger than the amount required to buy the virus, the price of the virus shall be deducted from user’s available golds and the virus shall be added to user’s virus library. if there are not enough golds available, the game shall present a warning to the user.
