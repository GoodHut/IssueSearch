 URL : "https://github.com/metarhia/metalog/issues/6" TITLE : log files naming BODY : we have 2 log source types: m - master, w - worker we have 2 logger types: s - server logs single per process , a - application logs for each app so we have combination: {m,s}, ~~{m,a}~~ application starts just in workers , {w,s}, {w,a} - {m,s} -> /log/yyyy-dd-mm-nodeid-impress.log , examples: - /log/2017-10-04-s1n0-impress.log - {w,s} -> /log/yyyy-dd-mm-nodeid-impress.log , examples: - /log/2017-10-04-s1n1-impress.log - {w,a} -> /applications/name/log/yyyy-dd-mm-nodeid-type.log , examples: - /log/2017-10-04-s1n1-metacom.log - /log/2017-10-04-s1n1-access.log - /log/2017-10-04-s1n1-slow.log
