 URL : "https://github.com/mozilla-l10n/pm-projects/issues/75" TITLE : define invitation criteria BODY : criteria for determining who we invite to the l10n workshop in berlin. here is some of the criteria that we've used in the past: - recent translation activity 3 months - leading/managing community l10n projects - new localizers with recent activity and a unique perspective - vocal participation in communication channels - testing pontoon this year, the l10n-drivers have decided to manage the full invitation process themselves by first creating the invite list for each community and then seeking the feedback of the community leader. if that can't be received in a reasonable timeframe, we will simply proceed to invite those we select based on the above criteria.
