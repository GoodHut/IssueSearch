 URL : "https://github.com/matrix-org/matrixmon/issues/1" TITLE : emergency slow mode BODY : in normal operation the defaults are interval=30s, send_deadline=5s, recv_deadline=20s. this gives a good granularity on the graphs. during high-stress scenarios, such as near-outage, it may be useful that the monitor can switch to a different mode of much higher interval and longer deadlines, so we get at least some visibility if the server is making progress just very slowly; trading off longer deadlines before giving up, with reduced granularity of time.
