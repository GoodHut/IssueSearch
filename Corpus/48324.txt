 URL : "https://github.com/OpenApoc/OpenApoc/issues/152" TITLE : rework vehicle firing BODY : right now vehicles firing is implemented in a strange way: - first we cycle through every equipment on vehicle to see if we have any functional weapon and remember biggest firing arc - then we look for closest enemy in the firing arc. - then we call attack this enemy - in which we again cycle through every equipment on vehicle and again confirm chosen enemy is within its firing arc instead we could just: - cycle through every equipment - if it's a weapon then - if we already know an enemy matching this firing arc - attack him - if not - find an enemy matching this firing arc - attack him
