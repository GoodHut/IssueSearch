 URL : "https://github.com/taylorholland/prj-rev-bwfs-dasmoto/issues/1" TITLE : filenames and organization BODY : browsers and servers usually expect a website to have an index.html file in the project's root directory. this is the entry point to your website or app. without this entry point, a web server may not know what file to serve on the initial request, resulting in: <img width= 450 alt= screen shot 2017-10-26 at 12 09 20 pm src= https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/7355203/32064005-a827ba4a-ba46-11e7-9443-743b9da1390b.png > so, moving forward make sure to name your main page index.html . the other pages can be named according to their purpose for example, product.html , contact.html , etc. but the entry point should simply be index.html . you should also have folders to organize your different assets. a css folder for your stylesheet s , an images folder for your images, etc.
