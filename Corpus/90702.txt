 URL : "https://github.com/NoImageAvailable/CombatExtended/issues/27" TITLE : spread out pawns in raids BODY : all pawns currently take the same path and they usually end up sharing all the suppression fire leading to disastrous results. a simple improvement could be made by changing the cost function of the a algorithm in raid mode to apply negative values to tiles that are near other pawns. this should naturally give pawns an incentive to spread out a bit more, kinda flocking.
