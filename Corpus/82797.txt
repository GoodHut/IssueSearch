 URL : "https://github.com/libretro/picodrive/issues/64" TITLE : associate with .68k files BODY : sega mega drive & genesis classics on steam provides some legal roms as .68k files. unfortunately, .68k is not recognized by retroarch as a valid extension for sega genesis roms. it is possible to play these i tested this on the usa version of sonic 3d blast by renaming the file extension from .68k to .bin but i think that the ideal behavior should be to support .68k files directly as a valid filename extension for sega genesis roms. this should be a trivial change.
