 URL : "https://github.com/jdemetra/jdemetra-app/issues/308" TITLE : bug in modelling BODY : we found a small bug in the modelling process of jdemetra+ v2.1.0. we use tramo, trfull as automatic process. the out of sample test was bad in the model found automatically, thus we put an outlier in it by hand. after this jdemetra put trading day, leap year and easter effect to the model. we eliminated these regressors from the model due to some theoretical reasons, and everything was fine. we saved the model and closed jdemetra. after we added an additional data, and opened the workspace again, and it showed the eliminated regressors in the model. after we push the refresh button the problem disappears, and everything goes on well.
