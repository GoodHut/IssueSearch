 URL : "https://github.com/michiya/django-pyodbc-azure/issues/124" TITLE : django 2.0 support BODY : django 2.0 is not supported. removing the version warning and trying to define pyodbc connections results in: django.core.exceptions.improperlyconfigured: 'sql_server.pyodbc' isn't an available database backend. try using 'django.db.backends.xxx', where xxx is one of: 'mysql', 'oracle', 'postgresql', 'sqlite3'
