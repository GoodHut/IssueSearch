 URL : "https://github.com/dear-github/dear-github/issues/257" TITLE : team level webhooks BODY : rather than having just having organisation and repository level webhooks. a very useful addition would be to have team webhooks. use case: - a slack integration on the org level will get lots of messages specific teams don't care about - having to integrate a slack on a per repository basis can get very tedious, especially when there are possibly many team members who can create repositories
