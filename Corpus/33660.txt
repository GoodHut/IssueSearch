 URL : "https://github.com/DocNow/docnow/issues/66" TITLE : alexa top 500 filter BODY : via @tjowens: it could be useful to look at the urls through a filter of the alexa top 500 https://www.alexa.com/topsites . this would allow a curator to possibly look for content that might not otherwise be picked up by a webarchiving service like internet archive. the only danger here is that if curators don't look at the other content they could be missing out. for example there are lots of twitter, youtube and instagram urls that will not be archived, even though those websites are in the alexa top 500.
