 URL : "https://github.com/StarttechVentures/Library/issues/68" TITLE : the lean startup 5/8 BODY : the lean startup: how today's entrepreneurs use continuous innovation to create radically successful businesses most startups fail. but many of those failures are preventable. the lean startup is a new approach being adopted across the globe, changing the way companies are built and new products are launched. eric ries defines a startup as an organization dedicated to creating something new under conditions of extreme uncertainty. this is just as true for one person in a garage or a group of seasoned professionals in a fortune 500 boardroom. what they have in common is a mission to penetrate that fog of uncertainty to discover a successful path to a sustainable business.
