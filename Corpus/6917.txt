 URL : "https://github.com/HitItAll/prj-rev-bwfs-dasmoto/issues/1" TITLE : classes vs id's BODY : https://github.com/hititall/prj-rev-bwfs-dasmoto/blob/master/dasmotoart-craft/index.html l10-l27 it seems you assigned classes to your brushes, frames and paint elements when you should have assigned id's to them. while classes are better for styling groups of elements the same way, id's are more appropriate for when you want to style unique, individual elements which is the case here for the brushes, frames and paint elements. to read more about the difference between class/id, please check out this resource: https://css-tricks.com/the-difference-between-id-and-class/
