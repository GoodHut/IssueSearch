 URL : "https://github.com/ymcatwincities/openy/issues/415" TITLE : news content type BODY : we would like to see a new content type news as part of openy. this could be an optional module, which will be enabled only if a client wants to have news on their website. the module should provide next features: - news content types, similar to blog but without style options. - paragraph to list all news - paragraph to list news by specific taxonomy term - paragraph to show the latest news - paragraph to show the latest news by a branch - paragraph to show the latest news by a camp - featured news
