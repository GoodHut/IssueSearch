 URL : "https://github.com/Stuart-Mayhew/f1-3-c2p1-colmar-academy/issues/5" TITLE : image alt attributes BODY : https://github.com/stuart-mayhew/f1-3-c2p1-colmar-academy/blob/master/colmar%20academy/index.html l93-l125 make sure to give your img tags an alt attribute. give it a value that adequately describes the image, just in case your website is not able to load it. doing so will help improve the accessibility of your website, which might be accessed by visually impaired users on e-readers or other devices that might deliberately neglect images-- as well as any other time that your website is just unable to render images.
