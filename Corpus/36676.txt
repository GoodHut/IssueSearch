 URL : "https://github.com/Julth/Tea-Cozy/issues/5" TITLE : image alt attributes BODY : https://github.com/julth/tea-cozy/blob/master/index.html l38-l56 give your img tags an alt attribute and giving it a value that adequately describes the image, just in case your website is not able to load it. doing so will help improve the accessibility of your website, which might be accessed by visually impaired users on e-readers or other devices that might deliberately neglect images-- as well as any other time that your website is just unable to render images.
