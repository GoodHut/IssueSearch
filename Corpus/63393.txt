 URL : "https://github.com/open-lambda/open-lambda/issues/24" TITLE : don't spawn a goroutine BODY : in the multiple places that we used a named pipe for ready signals, we are spawning a goroutine to listen on it as the worker goroutine waits. we should just do the listen in the worker thread instead. additionally, this code should be reused from a common place rather than being copy-pasted to the different locations.
