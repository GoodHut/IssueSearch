 URL : "https://github.com/sofa-framework/sofa/issues/221" TITLE : ci zombie test jobs BODY : we regularly have build slaves not responding on centos, windows and macos. after investigations, this is always caused by zombie test processes compliant_test that disturb the daily cleaning job, and therefore the whole build machine. the only way to fix it for a while is to kill all proccesses on the corresponding slave, wipe the work folder and restart the slave.
