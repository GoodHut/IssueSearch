 URL : "https://github.com/RoboCupAtHome/RuleBook/issues/273" TITLE : performance limits of spl BODY : this year is the first time for us to have standard platform leagues. hsr & pepper have some performance limits. please see the detail following, hsr - max motion speed: 0.8 km/h -> the walking operator should walk slower than 0.8 km/h. - max step height: 5 mm -> there should be no step heigher than 5 mm on the floor. - shoulder height: 1030 mm -> the grasping object on the shelf should be lower than 1030 mm. http://www.toyota-global.com/innovation/partner_robot/family_2.html pepper - max motion speed: 2 km/h. - max step height: 15 mm. - top height: 1210 mm. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/pepper_ robot specifications i propose these information need to be mentioned in the rule book. in addition, the all task need to be designed considering these limits.
