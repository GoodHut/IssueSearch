 URL : "https://github.com/KrusnikViers/Zordon/issues/3" TITLE : basic user commands BODY : - /activity_list -- list all activities - /subscribe <name> -- subscribe for activity - /unsubscribe <name> -- unsubscribe from activity - /active -- become active and receive notifications - /dnd -- enter do not disturb status and supress notifications - /summon <name> -- call all subscribed users for activity - /will_join <name> -- accept activity invitation - /will_not_join <name> -- do not accept invitation and supress all notifications - /will_join_later <name> -- accept invitation and notify others about being late
