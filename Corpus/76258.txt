 URL : "https://github.com/binnnliu/CS-Tips/issues/5" TITLE : io blocking in goroutine BODY : when a goroutine performs a blocking, non multiplexable, i/o block device for instance ,it will be blocked, but none of the other goroutines will be blocked. physically, you will have at least one physical thread per blocked goroutine on system call, c code, etc ... , plus a number of threads to run non blocked goroutines i.e. gomaxprocs .
