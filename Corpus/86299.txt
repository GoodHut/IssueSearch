 URL : "https://github.com/conveyal/r5/issues/377" TITLE : holes in regional results BODY : we are now accounting for the distance of a linked point/cell away from the road it is linked to. our linking distance on the destination opportunity grid end is still quite high around 1km to account for businesses in the middle of big parking lots etc. however our linking distance on the origin end is only 300 meters see com.conveyal.r5.streets.streetlayer link_radius_meters . the distance should be identical to that on the destination end. currently we get jagged holes in our output that are visually disruptive and require a place to have an access road in osm to have nonzero accessibility. we don't want this linking distance to be too huge though, or we'll be calculating useless accessibility numbers out into the ocean.
