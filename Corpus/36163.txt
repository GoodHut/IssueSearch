 URL : "https://github.com/JPDSousa/rookit-data-model/issues/51" TITLE : get album from track BODY : description ------------- in rookit semantics, a track is not contained in a single album it can be included in multiple albums , and because of that, a track.getalbum was always avoided. however, to simplify the process of fetching albums from tracks, a track.getalbums should be implemented. the field that stores the album should not be stored in the database, though.
