 URL : "https://github.com/mawww/kakoune/issues/1600" TITLE : comment-block and haskell BODY : hi. block comments in haskell are {-like this-} . in comment.kak l120 https://github.com/mawww/kakoune/blob/master/rc/core/comment.kak l120 , the actual exec to comment is: exec -draft %{a${closing}<esc>i${opening}} which, with haskell's opening and closing, becomes: exec -draft %{a-}<esc>i{-} which adds block comments {-}like this- .
