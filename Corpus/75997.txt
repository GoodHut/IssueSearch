 URL : "https://github.com/NicoJuicy/Tagly/issues/78" TITLE : improve layout suggested BODY : as mentioned by a user: the problem with displaying a lot of written content is that it becomes very challenging to parse. there are two small updates you can make to improve the reading/browsing experience: - employ a stronger information/type hierarchy. you do have one in place, but you may want to make the headings a stronger weight so they stand out more. - even better, increasing the spacing between articles. here's a quick write-up i found on the value of white space: https://www.uxpin.com/studio/blog/ux-case-study-designing-whitespace-to-improve-conversions/
