 URL : "https://github.com/w3c/html/issues/1076" TITLE : proposed html5.3 timeline BODY : during the webplat tpac meeting https://www.w3.org/2017/11/09-webplat-minutes.html item02 , a proposed timeline for html5.3 was discussed. based on the discussions, a refined timeline has now been proposed: thu 14th dec 2017: publish the first public working draft fpwd tue 6th feb 2018: publish an updated working draft wd tue 13th mar: publish an updated wd thu 24th may: publish candidate recommendation cr thu 12th jul: publish proposed recommendation pr thu 23rd aug: publish recommendation the intent is to ship within the current webplat charter https://www.w3.org/2017/08/webplatform-charter.html which ends on 30th september 2018. comments welcome.
