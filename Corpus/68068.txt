 URL : "https://github.com/dragon-drop-scratch/dragon-drop-www/issues/94" TITLE : relative links in pages BODY : there are several links that use ./ or ../ . they should be removed, as they can cause lots of errors. for example: html <link rel= stylesheet href= ./essentials/style.css> shouldn't be used, instead use: html <link rel= stylesheet href= https://dragon-drop-scratch.github.io/dragon-drop-www/essentials/style.css >
