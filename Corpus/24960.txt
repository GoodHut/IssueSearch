 URL : "https://github.com/fabric8-ui/fabric8-planner/issues/2146" TITLE : update font family BODY : the typeface need to be updated to match proper font-family definitions. currently, it is specified as font-family: open sans; this should be updated to be font-family: 'open sans', sans-serif; in order to take into account browsers that may not have the primary font available to it. this will allow the browser to fall back to a default, if necessary.
