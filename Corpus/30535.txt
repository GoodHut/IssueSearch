 URL : "https://github.com/djrobstep/migra/issues/9" TITLE : postgres 10 support BODY : migra is going to need changes to fully support postgres 10's new features. in particular: - identity columns https://blog.2ndquadrant.com/postgresql-10-identity-columns/ - native partitioning https://wiki.postgresql.org/wiki/new_in_postgres_10 native_partitioning the bulk of the work will be making changes to schemainspect to detect the identity column and partitioning information.
