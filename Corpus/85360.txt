 URL : "https://github.com/parapluu/Concuerror/issues/172" TITLE : experiment with transdpor BODY : according to transdpor https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-642-30793-5_14 , if an scheduling is planned in order to reverse a race with a delivery event, one can ignore other races with the same delivery event. the intuition is that due to transitivity, new races will appear in future interleavings. source dpor will likely not benefit, as irrelevant events will still be initials. optimal dpor however, will add more branches in the wakeup sequence, even with 'observers'.
