 URL : "https://github.com/alanmitchell/csv-transfer/issues/2" TITLE : imports with underscores BODY : looks like the system only includes one underscore from a multi-scored column name and cuts out everything starting at the second one. for instance, solar_tundertankonefoot_f_avg from the data file would become solar_tundertankonefoot table . this could be problematic for people with campbell scientific files as their system automatically appends units to the end of the column name ex, _avg
