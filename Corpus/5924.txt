 URL : "https://github.com/Railcraft/Railcraft/issues/1274" TITLE : train detector BODY : while testing with forecaster, we found that there was no good way of detecting a train with a detector without using a locomotive detector. usually, there would always be a locomotive powering a train, but that might not always be the case in the case of some trains, you only want one signal if a detector finds a match, and keep that signal until the entire train has departed, other times you want a pulse for each. so what about an option in their gui to change this behavior?
