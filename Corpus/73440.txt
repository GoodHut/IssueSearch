 URL : "https://github.com/ajheat/MultimediaExams/issues/11" TITLE : create grading interface BODY : the teacher will have access to a list of ungraded test submissions and be able to assign a numerical grade to each question and provide comments. ideally, the teacher will not have access to the name of the student who submitted the test before she finalizes that student's grade. this is an anti-bias measure used in many existing gradebook applications.
