 URL : "https://github.com/spectrecoin/spectre/issues/5" TITLE : balance is zero on startup BODY : sometimes when starting the wallet, the balance will show up as zero. there is no known pattern for this happening. my system is debian x86, i don't know whether windows users are affected. this problem has been reported multiple times by different people. symptoms: total balance is shown as 0.00 xspec, no transactions are shown, under balances all 6 kinds of balance public, private, reserved, stake, unconfirmed, immature are shown as zero. when going to wallets -> receive , there are no receive addresses listed at all. possible solutions seem to be, restarting the wallet multiple times doesn't fix it reliably , rebooting the system, recompiling the wallet or setting up the wallet again from scratch and copying wallet.dat.
