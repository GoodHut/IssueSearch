 URL : "https://github.com/Dzhama/TheCozyTea/issues/2" TITLE : class naming conventions BODY : try to stay away from locational names and ordered names for your classes. you should always plan for your code having to change especially on the web. what if we want to add another section, now the main-bottom is no longer the bottom and we have to go and change that. try to think of sections and groups as components. modular self sustaining objects. .tea-section .location-section you can probably just use your id's for styling, those names are good. https://github.com/dzhama/thecozytea/blob/master/index.html l27
