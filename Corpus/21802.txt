 URL : "https://github.com/drone/drone/issues/2246" TITLE : grpc health checks BODY : currently agent connectivity to server can be terminated under certain conditions e.g. no jobs for a while and/or firewall/lb terminating idle connections . this results in jobs being triggered and sitting in pending until the agent is restarted and reconnected to the server. grpc health checks could help prevent/correct this issue and make the drone platform more stable in general. discussion regarding this in discourse: https://discourse.drone.io/t/0-8-1-agent-loses-connection-overnight/864/7 grpc health checks docs: https://github.com/grpc/grpc/blob/master/doc/health-checking.md
