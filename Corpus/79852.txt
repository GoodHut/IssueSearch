 URL : "https://github.com/shoemann/f1-3-c2p1-colmar-academy/issues/3" TITLE : subtlety - media queries BODY : you're off to a great start with your media queries. good idea to think about how to show / hide desktop and mobile sections as the page is resized. make sure everything that needs to hide on mobile view does so by using that desktop class. looks like the 6 courses should hide to make way for the 6 smaller mobile sections. a good technique for figuring out where to apply styling and how to use media queries is to slowly resize the page and look for areas that need help. look for images that appear stretched or distorted, text that starts spilling out of its container or becoming un-aligned in some way. use media queries and styling to fix those issues as they crop up.
