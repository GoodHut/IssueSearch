 URL : "https://github.com/NREL/openstudio-geometry-editor/issues/51" TITLE : retain space selection BODY : try to ensure that a space is always selected when in rectangle/polygon modes. it can be confusing to be in these modes and not have a space selected -- which means that you can't draw anything. some examples: - when switching stories, either retain the last selected space from when you were on that story or default to selecting the first space in the list. - when deleting a space, switch to selecting another space in the list the one right after/before it? - when clicking on a selected space in the list, don't deselect it
