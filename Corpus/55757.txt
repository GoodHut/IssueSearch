 URL : "https://github.com/2017-iEvoBio/organization/issues/37" TITLE : lightning talk: simbit BODY : lightning talk: simbit authors: @remimattheydoret abstract: simbit is a high performance forward-in-time population genetics simulation platform coded in c++. with simbit, you can simulate complex demography, plasticity, epistasis, qtl and other things. the main secrets of simbit relies into the use of bitwise operators and the fact that all memory allocation at the beginning of the simulation. simbit has very good error handling to ensure a user understand what was unclear about its input.
