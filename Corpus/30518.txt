 URL : "https://github.com/webfun-sept2017/Sarah.Grayer.webfun-2017/issues/1" TITLE : portfolio - grading BODY : overall, this looks pretty good! make sure that as you're coding, you're paying attention to the intellisense red and green squiggly lines. there are some typos in your css that, when corrected, change your styling quite a bit. a missing colon or a semicolon where there should be a comma are small things, but they can make a big difference. when using float you don't need to set the display to inline-block because float will override it. text in the headers of your 'about' and 'projects' sections should be white, not black.
