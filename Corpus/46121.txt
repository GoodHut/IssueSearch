 URL : "https://github.com/qgis/homebrew-qgisdev/issues/33" TITLE : libzip in qgis master BODY : there is a new dependency in qgis master about libzip , this is what i had to do: brew install libzip ln -vs /usr/local/opt/libzip/lib/libzip/include/zipconf.h /usr/local/include cmake configuration libzip_include_dir= /usr/local/opt/libzip/lib/libzip/include libzip_library= /usr/local/lib/libzip.dylib
