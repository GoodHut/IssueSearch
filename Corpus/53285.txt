 URL : "https://github.com/woocommerce/woocommerce/issues/12893" TITLE : discount is misleading BODY : explanation of the issue when creating a coupon, the text box for basket discount allows % in the input, hence putting in 15% will make users think it's the basket % discount by mistake. this however deducts 15 pounds every time, and not 15% obviously. this has caused tremendous problems for us until we found the issue... luckily we didn't lose too much money before we fixed it. steps to reproduce the issue coupon->basket discount-> enter a % into the text box rather than just a pure number, it will accept it and not complain. suggested fix: please auto detect % and switch to basket % discount instead... or do not allow anything else other than just numbers and one decimal in the text box. thanks
