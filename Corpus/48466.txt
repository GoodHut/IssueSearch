 URL : "https://github.com/udacity/sdc-issue-reports/issues/407" TITLE : scikit-image hog quiz BODY : the scikit-image hog quiz in the vehicle detection and tracking lesson 19 only passes if transform_sqrt=true is passed to the hog function. this was nowhere specified as a requirement to pass the quiz and should perhaps be revised. the following invocation of hog causes the quiz to be marked as failed. however, adding the above flag causes it to be passed. features = hog img, orientations=orient, pixels_per_cell= pix_per_cell, pix_per_cell , cells_per_block= cell_per_block, cell_per_block , visualise=vis, feature_vector=feature_vec
