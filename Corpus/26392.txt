 URL : "https://github.com/elixir-lang/elixir-lang.github.com/issues/1000" TITLE : asciinema demos tip BODY : i just added ability to specify number of columns/lines to asciinema player when you're embedding from asciinema.org via <script> tag updated embedding docs https://asciinema.org/docs/embedding . for example, you can adjust the height of all demos in the recent 1.5 post by doing this: html <script type= text/javascript src= https://asciinema.org/a/iaok0yaztqdsujqn2sxa1frqw.js id= asciicast-iaok0yaztqdsujqn2sxa1frqw async data-rows= 15 ></script> notice added data-rows= 15 attribute, there's also data-cols= xxx for width by doing this you can make demos more compact in the blog post. you can test specific values by appending ?rows=15 to recording url: https://asciinema.org/a/iaok0yaztqdsujqn2sxa1frqw?rows=15 that's just a tip, feel free to ignore it :
