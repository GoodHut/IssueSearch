 URL : "https://github.com/castle-engine/view3dscene/issues/3" TITLE : default viewpoint at 0,0,0 BODY : in x3d scenes without an explicit viewpoint node, the initial camera position is at 0,0,0. however, the default x3d viewpoint position is at 0,0,10. it is easy to use the menu to navigate to the default x3d viewpoint but it took me a while to realize that there was not any problem with a scene itself, just the ciew3dscene initial camera position. here is an example: http://x3dom-integertrigger.glitch.me/integertrigger_test.x3d
