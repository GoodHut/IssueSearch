 URL : "https://github.com/Lordician/Tinkers-Fantasy/issues/8" TITLE : magic weapon: wand BODY : a wand used in practicing magic. notable features: - guards specify spell type. - focus crystal determines spell effect. - charged spell - if the user is swinging the weapon with a fully charged attack bar, the wand will cast it's spell. - focus item - able to empower spells cast from a spellbook. - dual wield - right clicking with this in the offhand will use this wand if possible .
