 URL : "https://github.com/IBMStreams/streamsx.nlp/issues/16" TITLE : ngram word count BODY : ngrams operator produces char sequence instead of words/terms. for example for ngrams size=2u and for input text= countries of the world , it produces co , ou , etc, instead of ngrams words like countries of . ngrambasic on the other hand work as expected and produces words. but the count functionality is not available in this operator. so either ngrams operator can be changed to produce words or ngrambasic can be extended to produce counts.
