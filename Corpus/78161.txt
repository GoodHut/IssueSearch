 URL : "https://github.com/unitystation/unitystation/issues/188" TITLE : playermove component 2.0 BODY : after reading through the photon authoritative server documents and taking the suggestions presented there, i've concluded the best approach for player movement would be this: remove the physicsmove component pulling out the rigidbody and then implementing the player move using transform.move functions. then placing a rigidbody2d object that follows each player and is only needed in the clients to be used for trigger detection. this way we do not have to give up on triggers and collision detectors i.e putting all that onto the matrix which will give us best of both worlds and is a lot easier to develop with.
