 URL : "https://github.com/DynaSim/DynaSim/issues/126" TITLE : studydir and compile_mode BODY : compile mode is broken by the following line in setupstudy around line 30 options.study_dir=fullfile options.project_dir, options.prefix '_' datestr now,'yyyymmddhhmmss' ; putting a time stamp on study_dir as above results in a separate solve folder being used for each simulation, forcing the compiler to re-compile on every run. for example, the solve folder becomes study_20170313023431/solve/params.mat
