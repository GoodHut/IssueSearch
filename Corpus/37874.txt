 URL : "https://github.com/langcog/childes-db-website/issues/13" TITLE : need citation policy BODY : on about page: citation policy if you use childes-db to access childes in your research, please cite: 1. childes itself – both the database and the corpora you use – following the talkbank policy http://talkbank.org/share/rules.html . 2. the childes-db paper, which is currently in prep: > sanchez , a., meylan, s., braginsky, m., macdonald, k., yurovsky, d., & frank, m. c. in prep . childes-db: a flexible and reproducible interface to the child language data exchange system childes . manuscript in preparation.
