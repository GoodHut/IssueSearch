 URL : "https://github.com/JuliaComputing/Deprecations.jl/issues/4" TITLE : space out ternaries BODY : omitting a space between the condition and the question mark in ternaries is deprecated as of https://github.com/julialang/julia/pull/22523 0.7.0-dev.797 . further, as of https://github.com/julialang/julia/pull/22712 0.7.0-dev.1004 , omitting spaces on either side of ? and : is deprecated in ternaries. it would be great if this was something that could be recognized and handled appropriately.
