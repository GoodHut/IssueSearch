 URL : "https://github.com/caskroom/homebrew-cask/issues/40711" TITLE : cask request: termhere BODY : cask details name: termhere homepage: https://hbang.ws/apps/termhere download url: https://github.com/hbang/termhere/releases/download/1.2.1/termhere.1.2.1.dmg description: termhere is a macos service and finder extension that has one job – to place a “new terminal here” button in the finder toolbar and in right-click context menus. click it, and a new terminal window will open that is immediately switched to the current directory, ready for you to work in immediately. note: this program does require being enabled in the extensions preference pane.
