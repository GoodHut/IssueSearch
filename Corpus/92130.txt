 URL : "https://github.com/gdoney1701/COAWST-Test/issues/1" TITLE : velocities in coawst/roms BODY : @gdoney1701 , some extra work needs to be done to plot velocity vectors from coawst/roms. if you think of each grid cell as a square, the u component is located on the left and right side of the square, and the v component on the top and bottom. so you have to average the u and v to the grid cell center to get something co-located that you can plot. here's an example: https://github.com/rsignell-usgs/notebook/blob/master/roms/roms%20adriatic%20velocity%20cartopy.ipynb
