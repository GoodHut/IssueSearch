 URL : "https://github.com/CtrlGames/Wake/issues/13" TITLE : interactions with workers BODY : interacting with workers should take two forms. the first is before you find your translator in the ship. when you talk to, or interact with the worker, they should say unintelligible things that are obviously language of some kind. be generous with interactions, users should be able to do a lot with the workers. once you find the translator the unintelligible things become words that you understand and the workers will be able to give you advice, tell you about themselves, and all sorts of things.
