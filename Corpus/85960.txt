 URL : "https://github.com/DomenicDev/Crucified/issues/30" TITLE : generate grass on the fly BODY : the current branch 'grass' brings the new feature to generate grass for areas of the terrain where a grass texture is sprayed on. however, this generating process is done during initialization. furthermore, it is done for the whole scene. this takes a noticeable amount of time and memory. especially, for bigger scenes this might not be a good idea. to solve this problem we could implement the grass generation on the fly. while the player is walking through the game world existing grass batches are removed and new grass batches will be created. what do you think @8keep and @jvpichowski ?
