 URL : "https://github.com/liberapay/liberapay.com/issues/753" TITLE : virtual wallet for teams BODY : how donations to teams work is a little confusing. aside from explaining it better 752 , we could also modify them to be closer to personal donations, and in the process make team accounts seem more real . the idea is to implement a virtual wallet for teams. during payday, when the team has a leftover, the money would go into the team's virtual wallet. the funds wouldn't actually move, they would still stay in the donors' wallets, but they would be tagged in our database as being reserved for future use by the team's members. this approach would allow a collective to accumulate money without having to create a legal entity or join an existing one fiscal sponsorship . thoughts?
