 URL : "https://github.com/NixOS/nixpkgs/issues/25380" TITLE : '?' in nix paths BODY : issue description bash scripts in nixpkgs are often assume that the first three segments of nix store paths /nix/store/$hash-$name of /nix/store/$hash-$name/bla bla bla do not have to be escaped, that they cannot contain spaces, quotes, cyrillic or characters which bash can interprets as wildcards ? { } . but ? is not forbidden and you can find quite a few with ls -l /nix/store | grep \? there is no real issue yet, and ? is the safest among the wildcards it matches any character including ? itself , but if would be safer to explicitly forbid creation of such paths. steps to reproduce ls -l /nix/store | grep \?
