 URL : "https://github.com/nicolanemarie/prj-rev-bwfs-dasmoto/issues/3" TITLE : group selectors in css BODY : simplify! i would consider adding a group selector for any repeated styles in your css. for example, font-family: helvetica; is repeated a few times throughout your css. instead of repeating the style you should use a group selector for it to make your css more concise. you could use: h1, h2, .price { font-family: helvetica; } then delete all instances of font-family: helvetica; from those individual selectors.
