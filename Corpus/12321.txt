 URL : "https://github.com/JessieArr/RelativePlacement/issues/1" TITLE : number of judges BODY : i posted this to your blog, but it hasn't appeared in the comments yet. if i have more time, i will give more feedback about your rp implementation, but there is one thing i wanted to point out. it isn't necessary to have an odd number of judges for rp to work properly. there are contests in which an even number of judges are used, such as those having at least one member of the couple judging each of the other couples, and there are an even number of couples. a contest with an even number of judges could result in ties that could be broken by a chief judge, such as the following: 1 1 1 3 3 3 3 3 3 1 1 1 2 4 4 4 4 4 4 2 5 2 5 2 5 5 2 5 2 5 the first two entrants are tied all the way out , including the pairwise tiebreaker.
