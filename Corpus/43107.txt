 URL : "https://github.com/bearing/radwatch-analysis/issues/41" TITLE : clearer roi variables BODY : the rois are currently defined using two variables, region_size and compton_distance : the width of each roi is currently 2 region_size fwhm . the separation between the center of the peak region and the center of a side region for normal cases is compton_distance fwhm - 2 region_size fwhm . let's make this clearer by rewriting all the roi channel calculations in terms of two new variables: region_width is the total width of each roi region. region_separation is the distance from the edge of the central region to the edge of a side region. both of these should still scale with fwhm as they currently do.
