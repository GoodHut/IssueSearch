 URL : "https://github.com/frc6357/robot_code/issues/31" TITLE : note: false ide errors BODY : if you find your ide throws a lot of red annotations with an error related to a missing super constructor for object , that's not bad code. it's a missing jre. see this link for how to fix it: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/17240708/implicit-super-constructor-object-is-undefined-must-explicitly-invoke-another make sure you use the jre under your jdk folder. mine was c:\program files\java\ jdk 1.8.0_112\jre
