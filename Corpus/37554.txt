 URL : "https://github.com/facg3/HMICoders-ToDoList/issues/15" TITLE : merging your own prs BODY : when working on a team, you should never merge your own prs. remember the gitflow workshop you did, it says it here: https://github.com/foundersandcoders/git-workflow-workshop-for-two step-9---programmer-2-merges-the-pull-request-1 https://github.com/foundersandcoders/git-workflow-workshop-for-two step-9---programmer-2-merges-the-pull-request-1 prs are pull requests : you are making a request to the other members of your team to review and merge the code. if your team members review the code, it means more people have an understanding of whats going on in the code, and also it reduces the chance of bugs being introduced. feel free to close this after your whole team has read it.
