 URL : "https://github.com/twpayne/go-kml/issues/5" TITLE : neatly indent coordinates BODY : currently <coordinates> are emitted on a single line, e.g. xml <coordinates>1,2,3 4,5,6 7,8,9</coordinates> it would be nice if coordinates, when emitted with writeindent were neatly indented, e.g. xml <coordinates> 1,2,3 4,5,6 7,8,9 </coordinates> this would required coordinates.marshalxml to know whether it is being called in pretty or compact node. this information is currently not exported by the standard encoding/xml library.
