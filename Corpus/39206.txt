 URL : "https://github.com/clinwiki-org/clinwiki/issues/58" TITLE : role & group support BODY : we need the ability to define user roles e.g. superuser, group admin, regular user . users should be able to interact with one or more groups i.e. switch between them group admins and superusers should be able to configure look and feel users working with a given group should be able to filter search results by studies that have been reviewed by their group. users looking at reviews for a given study should see reviews from users in their group first.
