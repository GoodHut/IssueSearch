 URL : "https://github.com/boazbk/tcs/issues/63" TITLE : chapter 16: nondeterminism BODY : i'd advocate adding a few more informal words about the concept of nondeterminism in the main body not just the bibliography as it does play an important role elsewhere in cs. many algorithms operate not deterministically but are also affected by other factors or inputs coming from their environment that the programmer may not have control over. asking whether it is possible for such an algorithm to have a particular behavior e.g. have some security failure on some computation path e.g. when an adversary controls some of the external inputs is an example of a nondeterministic computation of the kind captured by the class np.
